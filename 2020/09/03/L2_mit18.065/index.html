Lecture2: Multiplying and Factoring Matrices

MIT 18.065: Matrix Methods in Data Analysis, Signal Processing, and Machine Learning

this note is only a list of the key points

4 fundamental subspaces

matrixAm×nrank=r\quad A\quad m \times n\quad rank=rAm×nrank=r

4 fundamental subspaces notation dimension in
column space C(A)C(A)\quadC(A) dim=rdim=rdim=r Rm\R^mRm
row space C(AT)C(A^T)C(AT) dim=rdim=rdim=r Rn\R^nRn
nullspace N(A)N(A)N(A) dim=n−rdim=n-rdim=n−r Rn\R^nRn
left nullspace N(AT)N(A^T)N(AT) dim=m−rdim=m-rdim=m−r Rm\R^mRm

* row vectors have length n so row space is in Rn\R^nRn the others are similar

 
Nullspace: All solutions to Ax=0Ax=0Ax=0. i.e. all the vectors xxx satisfying Ax=0Ax=0Ax=0 forms the nullspace.

As we can see C(AT)C(A^T)C(AT) is orthogonal to N(A)N(A)N(A), C(A)C(A)C(A) is orthogonal to N(AT)N(A^T)N(AT).

e.g.

A=[124248]A=\begin{bmatrix} 1 & 2 & 4 \\ 2 & 4 & 8 \end{bmatrix} A=[12​24​48​]

AAA is 2×32\times32×3 and its rank=1rank=1rank=1.
Solve Ax=0Ax=0Ax=0 we can easily get that x=[0−21]or[40−1]x=\begin{bmatrix}0\\-2\\1 \end{bmatrix} or \begin{bmatrix}4\\0\\-1 \end{bmatrix}x=⎣⎡​0−21​⎦⎤​or⎣⎡​40−1​⎦⎤​. These two vectors form the N(A)N(A)N(A) as bases.
Since Ax=0Ax=0Ax=0, any row of AAA dot product any xxx equals 000.
i.e. any vector in C(AT)C(A^T)C(AT) dot product any xxx equals 000 and any row of AAA dot product any vector in N(A)N(A)N(A) equals 000.
i.e. C(AT)C(A^T)C(AT) is orthogonal to N(A)N(A)N(A)
