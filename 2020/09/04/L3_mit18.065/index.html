To creat orthogonal matrix, we have to find orthogonal bases. The eigenvectors of symmetric matrices (S=STS=S^TS=ST) and orthogonal matrices (QTQ=IQ^TQ=IQTQ=I) are orthogonal which helps us to build orthogonal matrices.
